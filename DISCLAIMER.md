## Key Management Warning

### ⚠️ CRITICAL: Keep Your Keys Safe!

- **Losing your encryption key means you CANNOT decrypt your data**
- There is NO key recovery mechanism
- There is NO backdoor
- The developers CANNOT help you recover lost keys

### Best Practices for Key Safety:

1. **Store keys securely** - Use a password manager or secure storage
2. **Create backups** - Keep encrypted backups of your keys
3. **Use strong passwords** - Protect your keys with strong passwords
4. **Don't share keys** - Only share with trusted parties
5. **Test decryption** - Verify you can decrypt before deleting originals
